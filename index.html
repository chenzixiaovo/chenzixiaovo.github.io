Bio.

I am currently a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Chentao Wu. Previously, I received my B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2022.

My current research interest includes computer architecture and memory hierarchy, with a focus on data prefetching and hybrid memory. Recently, I am exploring ways to leverage access locality to build efficient hybrid memory system.
